Welcome to Central Limestone Quarry Products Page

Central Limestone Company, Inc. is a crushed stone manufacturer supplying local markets with high quality aggregates. Our primary products are used as the foundation base in roads, parking lots, buildings and pipeline. Locally, we produce the highest quality coarse aggregate for Portland Cement Concrete. Our redi-mix customers can supply all state certified projects including those specifying the highest Illinois Department of Transportation (IDOT) quality designation, Class A Superstructure and Freeze Thaw.
